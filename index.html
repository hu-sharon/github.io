Sharon Hu

Product Designer

Hi! I'm an aspiring product designer who aims to create thoughtful, data-driven, and accessible designs. I developed a passion for accessible design after working with visually impaired users and motor impaired users. I learned about underrepresented communities and how design has the power to represent these individuals and empower them. I’ve also had experience working as a website designer for nonprofits and startups. I’ve also worked on legal products involved with divorce and in the healthcare industry.

Fun Facts: In my free time, I enjoy going to the gym, finding new music, and playing video games with friends.
